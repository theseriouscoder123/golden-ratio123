Golden Ratio in the Parthenon

The Parthenon is a former temple on the Athenian Acropolis, Greece, dedicated to the goddess Athena, whom the people of Athens considered their patron. It displays golden ratios in the height of the cross beams in relation to its height, and also in the rectangular designs below the sculptures.

Golden Ratio in the Notre Dame

The notre dame in paris has golden ratio proportions in a number of its key proportions of design.It reflects the golden proportions in the heights of each major stage of the structure as well as in the width of the columns at the top.

Golden Ratio in the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal displays golden proportions in the width of its grand central arch to its width, and also in the height of the windows inside the arch to the height of the main section below the domes.
