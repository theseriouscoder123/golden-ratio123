Golden Ratio in the Human Body

The golden ratio is present at many places in our feet. For instance-
1) The middle of the arch of the foot
2) The widest part of the foot
3) The base of the toe line and big toe
4) The top of the toe line and base of the “index” toe

Golden Ratio in the overall body

There is golden ratio in the body. For instance-
1) The distance between the belly button /distance between knee and bottom of the foot
2) The distance between top of head and the belly button /distance between the top of the head and shoulder
3) The distance between top of the head and shoulder /distance between the top of head and chin

Golden Ratio in the human lungs

One feature of the network of the bronchi that constitutes the lung is that itis asymmetric. For example, the windpipe divides into two main bronchi, one long(the left) and the other short (the right). This asymmetrical division continues into the subsequent subdivisions of the bronchi. It was determined that in all these divisions the proportion of the short bronchus to the long was always 1/1.618
